About Doctor

Dr. Amrita Jagdish Gotur has an overall experience of 11 years including 3 years in the field of neurology, specializing in the diagnosis and management of epilepsy, stroke, multiple sclerosis, dementia, movement disorders, ataxia, neuromuscular disorders, Neuro-immunological disorders and headache. She did her MBBS from Grant Government Medical College & Sir JJ Group of hospitals, Mumbai followed by M.D. (General Medicine) Topiwala National Medical College & BYL Nair Charitable Hospital, Mumbai and D.M. (Neurology) from All India Institute of Medical Sciences, New Delhi.


  • D.M. (Neurology) from All India Institute of Medical Sciences, New Delhi
  • M.D. (General Medicine) from Topiwala National Medical College & BYL Nair Charitable Hospital, Mumbai
  • M.B.B.S from Grant Government Medical College & Sir JJ Group of hospitals, Mumbai
