About

Vimhans Nayati

Vimhans Nayati Super Speciality Hospital, New Delhi is a compact, over 100+ bedded state-of-the-art centre with world-class infrastructure and a veteran team of highly-skilled doctors and nursing staff providing comprehensive medical care to patients from India and abroad. It offers best-in-class medical services in various super specialities, such as Neurology, Neurosurgery & Electro Physiology, Advanced Orthopaedics & Joint Replacement Surgery, Spine Surgery, Non-Invasive & Invasive Cardiology, Respiratory & Chest Medicine, Vascular & Endovascular Surgery, Gastroenterology, General Surgery, GI, MAS & Bariatric Surgeries, Oncology, Internal Medicine, Emergency & Trauma, Critical Care, ENT, Dermatology, Obs & Gynae, Ophthalmology, Aesthetic, Advance Rehabilitation Department . Vimhans Nayati is spread across four acres of a prime South Delhi locality with 5 state-of the-art, fully-integrated modular operation theatres and 36 intensive care beds.
