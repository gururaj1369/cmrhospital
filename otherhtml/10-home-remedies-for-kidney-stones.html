10 Home Remedies for Kidney Stones

Most doctors recommend that one should drink plenty of fluids to pass the kidney stones and prevent new stones from forming. The liquid helps flushing out the toxins and aids in moving the stones and grit through your urinary tract.

 

However, the water may not be sufficient to do the trick. You could add certain ingredients that could be beneficial to you. After drinking any flavored remedy, be sure to drink an 8 oz glass of water. This will help in moving the ingredients through your system.

 

Talk to your doctor for Urology Treatments in Delhi before getting started with any of the home remedies listed below. Your doctor can assess whether the home treatment is right for you, or it may lead to additional complications.

 

1. Water

If you want to pass the kidney stone, you can increase your water intake to speed up the process. Start drinking 12 glasses of water per day instead of the usual 8. You can continue to drink 8 to 12 glasses of water each day once the stone passes. The last thing you want to have is dehydration, which is one of the main risk factors for kidney stones, and you do not want the stones to form. Also, pay attention to the color of your urine. Your urine should be very light, pale yellow; if it's in dark yellow, then it's a sign of dehydration.

 

2. Lemon juice

You can also add freshly squeezed lemon to your water to change the flavor. Lemon contains citrate, which is a chemical that inhibits the formation of calcium stones. The stones get broken up due to the site rate, which enables them to pass more easily. To make a considerable effect, indeed, a great deal of lemon would be needed, but some can help a little.

 

3. Basil juice

Basil also contains acetic acid, which helps in the breakdown of the kidney stones and minimizes pain. Basil is full of nutrients and thus has been used traditionally for digestive and inflammatory disorders. Basil juice contains antioxidants and anti-inflammatory agents that help to maintain kidney health. You can use fresh or dried basil leaves to make a tree and drink several cups per day. Besides, you can also use fresh basil in a juicer or add it to your smoothie.

 

4. Apple cider vinegar

Apple cider vinegar also contains acetic acid, which helps in dissolving kidney stones. Apple cider vinegar helps in easing the pain caused by the stones besides flushing out the kidney stone. Numerous health benefits of Apple cider vinegar have been found. However, if Apple cider vinegar is ingested in larger amounts, it can lead to low potassium and osteoporosis levels. If you have diabetes, then you should exercise caution while drinking this mixture. Also, don't forget to monitor your blood sugar levels carefully throughout the day.

 

5. Celery juice

In traditional medications, celery juice has been thought to remove toxins that contribute to kidney stone formation. Celery juice helps in flushing out the body so that you could pass the stone. You could blend one or more celery stalks with water and drink the liquid throughout the day. Also, to know more about home remedies and kidney stone treatment in Delhi, visit the best hospital.

 

6. Pomegranate juice

For centuries pomegranate juice has been used to improve overall kidney function. The juice helps in flushing out the stones and other toxins from your system. Pomegranate juice helps keep the kidneys healthy and may have a role in preventing kidney stones from forming. Higher acidity levels in your kidney increase the risk for future kidney stones, and the pomegranate juice helps lower your urine acidity levels.

 

7. Kidney bean broth

Kidney beans cooked as a traditional dish are often used in India, which improves overall urinary and kidney health. The broth helps in dissolving and flushing out the stones. You have to cook the beans, strain the liquid from the beans and drink it throughout the day.

 

8. Dandelion root juice

Dandelion root juice is used as a kidney tonic. The juice is thought to help eliminate waste, enhance urine output, and improve digestion. That dandelions also contain Vitamin A, Vitamin B, Vitamin C, Vitamin D, and minerals such as potassium, iron, and zinc.

 

9. Wheatgrass juice

Wheatgrass juice is packed with many nutrients and has long been used to enhance health. It contains an essential nutrient that helps in cleansing the kidneys.

 

10. Horsetail juice

To flush out the kidney stones and soothe the swelling and inflammation, horsetail increases the urine flow. It also contains antibacterial and antioxidant properties that support overall urinary health. However, consult the doctor from the best hospital in Delhi because horsetail should not be used for more than six weeks at a time.
